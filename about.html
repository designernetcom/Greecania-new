About Us

Allow us to introduce ourselves if you haven't heard about Greecania yet! Greecania is a brand of Greeceania Mykonos Farmers Creamery Pvt. Ltd., a uniquely Greek-Indian company providing world-class authentic Greek food products. Our product range is ever-developing and evolving in the attempt to produce top-notch food items matching and surpassing European quality standards. We strongly believe in serving nothing but authentic Greek food experience to all Greek food lovers hence, we strictly adhere to our trade-protected Greek recipes.

Inspired by the age-old traditional ways of food making, with unique and original recipes all the way from Greece, we are happy to get expertise and investment from Greece as well India, making it a unique combination. Greecania is a world-class food product company on a mission to bring not only recipes and taste but also European quality standards, health consciousness, and Greek-food experience to India.

Why Choose Us?

India is a highly populated country where the production and consumption of dairy products are on a higher level, our Directors have decided to establish our new venture in India by the name of Greecania; a perfect blend of the Greek flavors to tickle the Indian taste buds. European standard of production technique, European machinery, and our passion for food has enabled our chain to serve India. So here we are, inviting you all to come and try authentic Greek food products blended with a modernized experience rooted deeply in the traditional taste. A healthy lifestyle, Greek taste, and world-class quality is what we stand for. We would love to welcome you all to the Greecania family, Greek in India!

To expand our horizons to serve more customers in fact, all of India we are now producing and distributing food products like Greek yogurt, authentic Greek cheeses, ice-creams, cheese, and puddings for all age groups, for everyone starting from babies to seniors. Not just that, our extensive menu with help of amazing packaging technology can now give you a plethora of aromatic food items like spreads, jams, sauces too. Some with their pungent tastes laced with delicate seasonings, and some extra light flavors elevated to high-class restaurant level of sophistication. You see, we are focused on giving an enhanced Greek food experience to all our customers through top-notch quality and authenticity. Greecania’s complete range is constantly expanding and we are proud of our strong stand on producing, packaging, and serving nothing but world-class authentic Greek recipes.

Mission And Vision

We are content with the purpose and mission of our company because we are contributing in the transformation of eating habits in India. As more and more people are choosing to be health conscious, we wish to encourage the same without compromising taste and experience. We are bound to innovate, re-discover, and serve people of all age groups and likings catering to everybody’s taste. We are constantly improving and setting higher standards for ourselves as well as the whole food industry. We are not only concerned about appearance and taste but also the content and experience that comes along with Greek food items.
